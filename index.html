About the Team

Established in 2025, the NTNU Seismic Design team is the first competition team focused on structural engineering at NTNU.

The Competition

We participate in the Seismic Design Competition (SDC) organized by EERI's Student Leadership Council. This prestigious international competition challenges university teams to design, analyze, and build a cost-effective and architecturally innovative earthquake-resistant structure.

The Challenge

Teams must design and construct a scaled balsa wood model of a multi-story tower that can withstand simulated ground motions on a shake table. The competition evaluates:

  • Seismic Performance
  • Innovation in Design
  • Architecture & Sustainability
  • Cost Efficiency

Portland 2026

The 2026 competition will be hosted in and designed based on Portland, USA. The design rules define a unique tower with negative space that poses difficult engineering challenges.
